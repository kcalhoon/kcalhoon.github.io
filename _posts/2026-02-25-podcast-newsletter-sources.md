I get asked how I stay on top of AI developments. Here's my 3-step system for tracking AI insights, plus my full curated list of top podcasts and newsletters.

Uncovering insights requires structure, not serendipity. Here is the framework I use to filter the AI ecosystem:

- **Curate Aggressively:** Limit inputs to high-authority voices.
- **Leverage Audio:** Podcasts offer off-the-cuff insights you won't find in written copy.
- **Automate Everything:** I run GitHub Actions to fetch, parse, and email me summaries daily.

My top five go-to sources span business strategy, news digests, technical research, and practical application:
